  • Time Management

  • Time management is a key factor throughout my career. It's been necessary in running activity groups for outdoor centres, and managing a business. Needless to say time management is fairly self explainitory, but regardless I've grown to realise throughout my professional career that without it, you tend not to last very long in a position.

  • I've worked for individuals before with little to no time management and it's given me the perspective and drive to operate in the opposite direction and focus on running a business as smoothly and efficiently as possible. When managing essentially three businesses in one with Saddles and Paddles, I'd often have to look ahead at the bookings for the day on a busy weekend and manage the best time frames in ensuring all aspects of the business are run effectively as possible both for staff management and also customer satisfaction.
